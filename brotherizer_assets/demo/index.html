What the slider is doing

It is not fake animation. Each stop moves the line from plain, to cleaned up, to more human, to fully brotherized. Same idea. Better pulse.

What actually changes

Rhythm, compression, surface fit, and how much the sentence sounds like it belongs in a reply, a note, or a product update instead of a model sandbox.

Why the styles differ

Brotherizer routes through donor texture, mode, surface, formatting pressure, and reranking. That is why the same sentence can come back casual, reflective, restrained, or sharper without losing the point.
